<p class="eyebrow">Update • Feb 2025</p>

<p class="lede">Goal: stop staring at the screen. We configured TradingView alerts that work with your eToro watchlist (stocks, indices, ETFs, crypto where allowed). Clone them, adjust risk, and get signals via app, email, or webhook.</p>
<section>
<h2>Quick prerequisites</h2>
<ul>
<li>TradingView Pro or above for multiple simultaneous alerts.</li>
<li>Correct timezone to avoid off-session triggers.</li>
<li>Risk sizing done before sending the eToro order.</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Alert templates to clone</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Breakout + volume</strong>: price breaks resistance, volume &gt; 20% above average.</li>
<li><strong>Clean pullback</strong>: price taps EMA20 after breakout, no full counter candle.</li>
<li><strong>Trailing stop</strong>: alert when price breaks last 1H low to tighten stops.</li>
<li><strong>Crypto (if available)</strong>: VWAP cross + RSI &lt; 60 in uptrend.</li>
</ul>
<p>We published these models with screenshots and parameters on <a href="https://tradingview-avis.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">our full TradingView guide</a>. Duplicate and swap tickers for your list.</p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Good practices for eToro</h2>
<ul>
<li>Only alert on assets available in your region (stocks/ETFs, crypto if allowed).</li>
<li>Avoid leverage on CFDs if you’re new; prefer unleveraged positions.</li>
<li>Set profit zone and a technical stop before placing the eToro order.</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Setup checklist</h2>
<ol>
<li>Create an “eToro” watchlist in TradingView.</li>
<li>Apply the “Breakout + volume” template to your key symbols.</li>
<li>Weekly review: clean expired alerts, adjust levels.</li>
<li>On eToro, use fractional sizing (stocks) or minimum crypto where allowed; avoid oversizing.</li>
</ol>
</section>
<section>
<h2>FAQ</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Do alerts execute trades?</strong> No, they only notify. You place the order on eToro.</li>
<li><strong>Do I need a paid TradingView plan?</strong> Yes, to keep multiple alerts live.</li>
<li><strong>Crypto unavailable on my eToro?</strong> Disable crypto templates; stick to stocks/ETFs.</li>
</ul>
