<section id="what-is-etoro">
<h2>1. What is eToro?</h2>
<p>Founded in 2007, eToro is a multi-asset investment platform that offers <strong>real stocks at 0% eToro commission</strong> (non-CFD), the ability to copy investors via <strong>CopyTrader™</strong>, <strong>Smart Portfolios</strong> for thematic exposure, and access to certain <strong>cryptoassets</strong> depending on your jurisdiction. The company operates through several regulated entities; the entity that serves you depends on your country of residence.</p>
<p class="note small">Key takeaway: eToro is a regulated intermediary. This does not guarantee the availability of specific products in your country, nor any future performance. Financial assets (including crypto) carry a risk of capital loss.</p>
</section>
<section id="regulations">
<h2>2. Regulation &amp; entities</h2>
<p>eToro (Europe) Ltd is authorised and regulated by the <strong>Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC)</strong> under license number <strong>109/10</strong>. eToro (UK) Ltd is authorised and regulated by the <strong>Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)</strong> in the United Kingdom under FRN <strong>583263</strong>. In Australia, eToro AUS Capital Limited holds AFSL <strong>491139</strong> and is supervised by <strong>ASIC</strong>. These details are published on eToro's official pages and regulator registers.</p>

<section id="availability">
<h2>3. Service availability by country</h2>
<p>The availability of products (e.g., cryptoassets), payment methods and certain features varies by jurisdiction. Local restrictions may apply (e.g., some countries do not allow the promotion or provision of crypto services). <section id="products">
<h2>4. Products &amp; features</h2>
<h3 id="stocks">4.1 Real stocks at 0% eToro commission</h3>
<p>eToro allows you to buy <strong>real stocks</strong> with <strong>0% eToro commission</strong> (<em>non-CFD</em>). Other costs may exist — such as currency conversion or market spreads. Always review eToro's "Fees" page for current conditions and regional differences.</p>
<p class="ref"><a href="/sources/etoro-en#fees" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Fees &amp; policies</a></p>
<h3 id="copytrader">4.2 CopyTrader™ (copy other investors)</h3>
<p>CopyTrader™ lets you <strong>automatically mirror</strong> the trades of a visible investor on the platform. You can set a <em>Copy Stop Loss</em> (a maximum loss threshold for the copy relationship) and add/remove funds to a copy. Copying doesn't guarantee performance; you can lose some or all of the funds allocated to the copy. Review the social-copying risk notice before using the feature.</p>

</ul>
<h3 id="smart-portfolios">4.3 Smart Portfolios</h3>
<p><strong>Smart Portfolios</strong> are thematic baskets constructed according to a defined methodology (technology, indices, sectors, etc.). There are <em>no eToro management fees</em> for Smart Portfolios, though underlying costs may exist and minimum investment amounts can apply. Read the documentation and methodology for each portfolio you consider.</p>

<h3 id="crypto">4.4 Cryptoassets (by jurisdiction)</h3>
<p>eToro provides access to certain cryptoassets in permitted jurisdictions. <strong>Since July 2025, eToro applies a transparent 1% commission at both open and close</strong>, shown <em>separately</em> from the market spread for greater clarity in trade performance and costs. Cryptoassets are volatile; ensure you understand local regulation and risks.</p>

<section id="fees">
<h2>5. Fees in detail (2025)</h2>
<p>Fees are a key driver of long-term results. Below are the main cost items to understand. Always check eToro's official fee pages and help articles for the latest details and regional differences.</p>
<h3>5.1 Real stocks</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>eToro commission:</strong> 0% for <em>real stocks</em> (non-CFD).</li>
<li><strong>Spreads:</strong> the bid/ask differential set by the market.</li>
<li><strong>Currency conversion:</strong> if your base currency differs from USD, FX conversion costs may apply.</li>
</ul>
<h3>5.2 Crypto</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Commission:</strong> 1% at open and 1% at close, <em>separate from spread</em> (since July 2025).</li>
<li><strong>Network fees:</strong> may apply for transfers to/from eToro Money depending on the chain/asset.</li>
</ul>
<h3>5.3 Deposits, withdrawals &amp; other</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Withdrawals:</strong> fixed and/or variable fees may apply depending on your region/currency.</li>
<li><strong>Inactivity fee:</strong> a monthly charge may apply after a defined period without login (e.g., 12 months).</li>
<li><strong>Overnight fees (CFDs):</strong> apply when you hold CFD positions overnight.</li>
</ul>

<section id="funding">
<h2>6. Deposits, withdrawals &amp; currencies</h2>
<p>Deposit methods (cards, bank transfer, e-wallets…), accepted currencies and minimums depend on your country and serving entity. Withdrawals are subject to identity verification (KYC) and the availability of methods in your region. Processing times may vary by method and intermediary banks.</p>

<section id="risks">
<h2>7. Major risks &amp; warnings</h2>
<ul class="risk-list">
<li><strong>CFDs:</strong> leverage can magnify losses; in the EU, <strong>61%</strong> of retail accounts lose money with this provider. <a href="/sources/etoro-en#risk" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">EU risk warning</a></li>
<li><strong>CopyTrader™:</strong> copying does not guarantee future results; you remain responsible for losses. <a href="/sources/etoro-en#copy-trading-risks" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Copying risks</a></li>
<li><strong>Cryptoassets:</strong> high volatility and evolving regulation. Confirm local restrictions and tax rules.</li>
<li><strong>FX risk:</strong> investing in assets not denominated in your base currency adds exchange-rate risk.</li>
</ul>
<p class="small note">This guide is informational only. It is not investment advice or a personalized recommendation.</p>
</section>
<section id="pros-cons">
<h2>8. Pros &amp; limitations</h2>
<div class="grid-2">
<div>
<h3>Pros</h3>
<ul>
<li>Modern, mobile-first interface that's approachable for beginners.</li>
<li>0% eToro commission on <em>real stocks</em> (non-CFD) and a broad asset lineup.</li>
<li>CopyTrader™ and Smart Portfolios enable learning by observing real-world strategies.</li>
<li>Increased transparency on crypto fees (1% commission shown separately from spread).</li>
<li>Regulatory oversight (CySEC/FCA/ASIC) and extensive documentation.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h3>Limitations</h3>
<ul>
<li>Product/feature availability varies by jurisdiction.</li>
<li>Market spreads and currency conversion costs require monitoring.</li>
<li>Copying strong past performers does not remove the risk of loss.</li>
<li>CFDs are not suitable for everyone due to the potential for rapid losses.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<section id="good-practices">
<h2>10. Responsible investing practices</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Plan</strong> your objectives and time horizons; avoid impulsive decisions.</li>
<li><strong>Diversify</strong> across asset classes and sectors; never invest money you need in the short term.</li>
<li><strong>Monitor fees</strong> (conversion, withdrawals, crypto commissions) and market spreads before each order.</li>
<li><strong>Understand copying</strong>: an investor's track record is not predictive. Configure Copy Stop Loss.</li>
<li><strong>Security</strong>: enable account protection, keep your devices updated and beware of phishing.</li>
</ul>
