We prioritize **primary sources** whenever possible. Since platforms evolve, the most accurate information is often what you see right before confirming an action.

## Source types

- official pricing/fees pages
- terms and contractual documentation
- official help centers (deposit, withdrawal, verification, restrictions)
- risk disclosure pages

## Quick verification checklist

Before acting, double-check:

- the **fee recap** at confirmation (conversion, withdrawal, spread)
- **country restrictions** (products, limits, payment methods)
- **terms** (inactivity, minimums, delays)
