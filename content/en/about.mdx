avis-invest.com publishes **practical guides** (and comparisons) to help you understand platforms like eToro or Bitpanda: fees, how they work, key risks, and simple verification steps.

We follow a checklist mindset: the goal is to avoid common mistakes (unexpected fees, withdrawal friction, misunderstanding risk).

## Affiliate disclosure

Some pages include **affiliate links**. If you use these links, we may earn a commission (at no extra cost to you).  
This helps fund maintenance (writing, updates, hosting).

Our guidelines:

- no “get rich quick” promises
- focus on **risks** and real-life friction (fees, conversions, withdrawals)
- process-first recommendations, not “tips”

## Updates & corrections

Platforms change often (pricing, terms, country restrictions).  
When something important changes, we update the relevant page and display an update date.

If you spot an error, message us and we’ll fix it.

## Important disclaimer

Content on avis-invest.com is **informational and educational**. It is not financial, legal or tax advice.  
Investing and trading involve risk, including partial or total loss of capital.
