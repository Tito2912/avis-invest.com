<h2>Products &amp; services (availability depends on country)</h2>
<div class="grid-3">
<article class="card">
<h3>Crypto (buy/sell/swap)</h3>
<p>Bitpanda states the exact cost is shown before confirmation. Fees can vary by asset (stated range: <strong>0.00% to 2.49%</strong>). Example: Bitcoin premium <strong>0.99%</strong> on buy and sell.</p>
</article>
<article class="card">
<h3>Stocks &amp; ETFs (Real Securities)</h3>
<p>Real ownership, orders executed on a regulated exchange during market hours. Bitpanda states it does not charge commissions, but a <strong>spread</strong> (bid/ask) applies.</p>
</article>
<article class="card">
<h3>Precious metals</h3>
<p>Gold, silver, platinum and palladium (physical metals, per Bitpanda). Premiums apply (e.g., gold: <strong>0.5%</strong> buy / <strong>1%</strong> sell) and Bitpanda states there are no storage/holding fees.</p>
</article>
<article class="card">
<h3>Bitpanda Card</h3>
<p>Visa card (depending on eligibility) to spend your assets. Bitpanda states ATM withdrawals cost <strong>2%</strong> of the withdrawal (minimum <strong>€2</strong>) and each payment triggers an in‑app conversion (trade) with the usual trading fees.</p>
</article>
<article class="card">
<h3>Savings plans</h3>
<p>For recurring purchases, Bitpanda offers savings plans (stated minimum: <strong>€10</strong> or equivalent).</p>
</article>
<article class="card">
<h3>Security (best practices)</h3>
<p>Enable <strong>2FA</strong>, monitor active sessions and stay alert to phishing. Bitpanda says it stores the <strong>vast majority</strong> of crypto assets offline (cold storage).</p>

<h2>How to get started</h2>
<ol class="steps">
<li>Check eligibility (country of residence, accepted documents) and create an account.</li>
<li>Complete identity verification (KYC) and, if requested, provide supporting documents (proof of funds/wealth).</li>
<li>Secure your account: unique password + 2FA + review devices/sessions.</li>
<li>Deposit funds (stated minimum: €10 or equivalent), then review the fee summary.</li>
<li>Start with an amount that fits your profile (stated crypto minimum: €1 or equivalent).</li>
<li>Monitor exposure and avoid over-trading when spreads/costs are unfavorable.</li>
</ol>
<div class="grid-2">
<article class="card">
<h3>Security checklist</h3>
<ul class="ref-list">
<li>Enable 2FA right away (authenticator app).</li>
<li>Review active sessions and sign out unknown devices.</li>
<li>Avoid links from messages: type the URL yourself.</li>
</ul>
</article>
<article class="card">
<h3>Fees checklist</h3>
<ul class="ref-list">

<li>Compare: spreads/premiums + deposit/withdrawal fees depending on payment method.</li>
<li>For securities (stocks/ETFs), remember <strong>market hours</strong> and that a <strong>spread</strong> (bid/ask) can apply.</li>

<p>No. Products and availability can differ depending on regulation and residency.</p>
