<section class="faq">
<div class="container">
<h2>FAQ</h2>
<details>
<summary>Is eToro regulated?</summary>
<p>eToro operates via several regulated entities (EU: CySEC, UK: FCA, AU: ASIC). The entity serving you depends on your country of residence.</p>
</details>
<details>
<summary>Are stocks really at 0% commission?</summary>
<p>Yes for <em>real stocks</em> (non-CFD). Other costs can exist (FX conversion, market spreads, etc.).</p>
</details>
<details>
<summary>Can I copy other investors?</summary>
<p>Yes via CopyTrader™. Copying is not a guarantee of results; you can lose money.</p>
</details>
<details>
<summary>What are the main risks?</summary>
<p>Asset prices can go down as well as up. CFDs carry high risk: <strong>61% of retail accounts lose money</strong> with this provider.</p>
</details>
</div>
