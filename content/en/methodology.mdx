Our goal is simple: help you **understand** a platform and **verify** the critical points before you deposit money.

## What we check (checklist)

Depending on the platform (broker, crypto exchange, tool), we usually review:

- **Fees**: spreads, commissions, inactivity, conversion, withdrawals, overnight (when applicable)
- **Products / risks**: CFDs, leverage, crypto, stocks/ETFs, country restrictions
- **Safety & compliance**: KYC, basic protections, risk disclosures, terms transparency
- **Deposits / withdrawals**: methods, delays, limits, fees, common friction
- **UX**: interface, features, support, known limitations

## Sources

We prioritize primary sources:

- official pricing/fees pages
- terms and official documentation
- help centers (deposit/withdrawal, verification, restrictions)

When possible, we link to the source directly in the article.

## Updates

Pages that impact “real money” (fees, withdrawals, terms) are prioritized for updates.  
If you spot an inconsistency, send the page URL and what should be corrected.
