Products & services (availability depends on country)

Crypto (buy/sell/swap)

Bitpanda states the exact cost is shown before confirmation. Fees can vary by asset (stated range: 0.00% to 2.49%). Example: Bitcoin premium 0.99% on buy and sell.

Stocks & ETFs (Real Securities)

Real ownership, orders executed on a regulated exchange during market hours. Bitpanda states it does not charge commissions, but a spread (bid/ask) applies.

Precious metals

Gold, silver, platinum and palladium (physical metals, per Bitpanda). Premiums apply (e.g., gold: 0.5% buy / 1% sell) and Bitpanda states there are no storage/holding fees.

Bitpanda Card

Visa card (depending on eligibility) to spend your assets. Bitpanda states ATM withdrawals cost 2% of the withdrawal (minimum €2) and each payment triggers an in‑app conversion (trade) with the usual trading fees.

Savings plans

For recurring purchases, Bitpanda offers savings plans (stated minimum: €10 or equivalent).

How to get started

  1. Check eligibility (country of residence, accepted documents) and create an account.
  2. Complete identity verification (KYC) and, if requested, provide supporting documents (proof of funds/wealth).
  3. Secure your account: unique password + 2FA + review devices/sessions.
  4. Deposit funds (stated minimum: €10 or equivalent), then review the fee summary.
  5. Start with an amount that fits your profile (stated crypto minimum: €1 or equivalent).
  6. Monitor exposure and avoid over-trading when spreads/costs are unfavorable.

Security checklist

  • Enable 2FA right away (authenticator app).
  • Review active sessions and sign out unknown devices.
  • Avoid links from messages: type the URL yourself.

Fees checklist

  • Compare: spreads/premiums + deposit/withdrawal fees depending on payment method.
  • For securities (stocks/ETFs), remember market hours and that a spread (bid/ask) can apply.

Security & compliance: concrete points

What Bitpanda states

  • Offline storage (cold storage) for the vast majority of crypto assets.
  • Session/device management and extra confirmations for sensitive actions.
  • Custodial service: you typically don’t access private keys through the app.

What you should do

  • Enable 2FA + unique password + secure email.
  • Review connected devices/sessions regularly.
  • Stay alert to phishing and fake “support”.

KYC / documents

  • Identity verification (KYC) is required.
  • In some cases, Bitpanda may request proof of funds / proof of wealth documents.
  • Responding to compliance requests helps avoid deposit/withdrawal blocks.

Insurance (eligibility-based)

  • Bitpanda describes an insurance program for eligible digital assets held in custody (conditions/pro‑rata).
  • Check exclusions, caps and scope before relying on it.

Exact measures depend on products and your country. Check Bitpanda help pages for up-to-date details.

Bitpanda FAQ

Is it suitable for beginners?

Generally yes. Still, take time to understand fees and risks before investing.

Should I enable 2FA?

Yes. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) right after signup and avoid password reuse.

Is the offer the same in every country?

No. Products and availability can differ depending on regulation and residency.

What fees should I expect on crypto?

Bitpanda states the cost is shown before confirmation and can vary by asset (stated range: 0.00% to 2.49%). Check the trade summary before confirming.

Does Bitpanda offer stocks and ETFs?

Yes, via Real Stocks & ETFs (Real Securities) depending on your country. Bitpanda states orders are executed on a regulated exchange with market hours (Mon–Fri 07:30–23:00, not 24/7) and that a spread (bid/ask) can apply.

What is the minimum amount to start?

Bitpanda states a minimum of €10 (or equivalent) for fiat deposits/withdrawals and €1 (or equivalent) for crypto buy/sell/swap.

Who can order the Bitpanda Card?

Bitpanda states the card is available in eurozone countries (with exceptions such as Cyprus and Liechtenstein) and requires a verified account and the mobile app to manage card features.

Can Bitpanda request proof of funds documents?

Yes. In some cases, Bitpanda may request proof of funds / proof of wealth documents as part of compliance obligations.
